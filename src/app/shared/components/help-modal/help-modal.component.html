Mastering Clock In & Out

Follow these 3 easy steps every day to ensure your records are valid:

Step 1: Share GPS Location

When you click "Clock In", your browser will ask for location permission. Click **"Allow"**. You must be within the university zone to successfully save your record.

Step 2: Digital Signature

Draw your signature clearly on the screen using your mouse or touch screen. Click "Clear" if you make a mistake and want to start over.

Step 3: Save and Confirm

Once signed, hit **"Save Signature"**. A success message will appear, and the dashboard timer will start counting your session.

Forget to Clock Out? The system will keep your session "Active". You must clock out immediately upon return before starting a new session.

Troubleshooting Hub
System says "Location Access Denied"?

Click the lock icon in your browser's address bar, reset permissions for this site, and refresh the page. Ensure your device's global GPS is ON.

Why can't I see my intern's reports?

Ensure the intern is correctly assigned to your Department in their profile settings. Admins can verify these links in the User Management portal.

How do I generate a Monthly Report?

Navigate to the "Reports" module, select the desired month, and click "Export PDF". This will include all verified signatures for the period.

I was bulk-imported. What do I do?

Welcome! First, go to Settings > Profile and upload your signed Internship Agreement (PDF or Image). This is required for valid attendance tracking.

Session timeout issues?

For security, sessions expire after 24 hours of inactivity. Simply log back in to refresh your token.
